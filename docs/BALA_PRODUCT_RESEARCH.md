# BALA Product Research Notes

## Inspiration: Neko Health-style experience

Important: BALA must not copy Neko Health branding, wording, clinical claims, scan product, diagnostic positioning, or medical clinic model.

Neko Health is a clinic-led full-body scan service. Their public product journey includes a body scan, sensor/blood measurements, quick results, doctor consultation, and app-based health data tracking. Their official pages describe proprietary sensors, blood analysis, on-site results, and a medical professional consultation. Neko also emphasizes tracking change over time and giving users a health summary in an app.

BALA is different:
- BALA is not a clinic.
- BALA does not perform scans.
- BALA does not analyze blood.
- BALA does not diagnose disease.
- BALA does not detect medical conditions.
- BALA does not replace doctors.
- BALA does not provide emergency monitoring.

What BALA can safely learn from this style:

1. Clear health journey
   User should understand: add/import signals → see today’s score → compare with baseline → understand what changed → optionally share a doctor-ready summary.

2. Baseline-first experience
   Instead of only showing today’s numbers, BALA should help users build a personal baseline from recent check-ins.

3. Track change over time
   BALA should show simple trend language:
   - near baseline
   - below baseline
   - better than baseline
   - changed from recent pattern

4. Doctor-ready summaries
   BALA should help users organize their own signals and symptoms into a clean summary they can discuss with a healthcare professional.

5. Premium calm UX
   Use clean cards, simple labels, calm copy, and no fear-based alerts.

6. Trust and boundaries
   BALA should clearly say it is for personal health awareness, not diagnosis or emergency use.

## Stage 3 product direction

Create “Baseline & Trends”:
- Build baseline from last 3 valid check-ins.
- Compare latest signals to baseline.
- Show “What changed?”
- Add Doctor-Ready Timeline with last 5 check-ins.
- Update Ask BALA Coach to use baseline/trend data.
- Keep language safe, calm, and non-diagnostic.

## Safe BALA language

Use:
- patterns
- baseline
- signals
- awareness
- check-in
- recent trend
- recovery
- balance
- discuss with a healthcare professional if concerned

Avoid:
- detect disease
- diagnose
- predict
- prevent
- emergency monitoring
- early warning signs
- replace doctor
