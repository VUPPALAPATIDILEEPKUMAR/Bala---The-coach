Privacy

Your health data should remain yours

BALA's browser mode stores manual entries and imported Apple Health summaries in local browser storage. The imported file is processed locally and is not uploaded.

Embedded coach

BALA’s embedded coach uses local rules and a small built-in wellness knowledge base. Questions, metric summaries, Apple Health exports, record history, names, emails, and device identifiers are not sent to an AI provider.

Spoken replies use the device’s installed text-to-speech voices. Voice input uses the browser’s speech-recognition feature, which may process microphone audio through Apple, Google, Microsoft, or another browser service depending on the device. BALA does not store the recording.

Connected wearable services

Future HealthKit, Health Connect, Fitbit, Samsung Health, Oura, and Garmin connections must request the minimum permissions needed, retain source provenance, and provide deletion and revocation controls.

Safety

BALA provides wellness information and does not diagnose conditions, provide emergency monitoring, or replace professional medical care.

Delete your browser data

Use Clear local data inside BALA. Removing the installed app or clearing site storage also deletes browser-local BALA data.
