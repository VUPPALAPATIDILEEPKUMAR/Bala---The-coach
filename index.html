Why this score?

BALA Score is a reflection guide based on available check-in signals. It does not provide diagnosis and is not for emergency decisions. It reflects only the signals you have shared — such as sleep, recovery, heart-rate signals, and activity. Data confidence depends on how many supported signals were available today. For urgent symptoms such as chest pain or pressure, trouble breathing, fainting, severe weakness, or stroke-like symptoms, seek urgent care or contact local emergency services. Do not rely on the BALA Score for emergency decisions.
